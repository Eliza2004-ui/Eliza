My Education

I have completed my junior high school studies and am about to graduate from high school. I look forward to going to Ningbo City Technical College.

Because there are enough excellent teachers and resources here to cultivate me into an excellent designer.My classmates and I helped each other and achieved good grades under the strict guidance of the teacher.Through continuous exercise and effort, learn basic courses with a down-to-earth approach,I expect to become a graphic designer .

My hobbies
I am a lively and outgoing girl. In my free time, I enjoy drawing. I believe that slowly filling a blank piece of paper with my own work is a satisfying thing for me. I also enjoy listening to music, especially some light music, because it can make me feel relaxed.
My Career
Three years ago, I arrived at Ningbo Foreign Affairs College. At that time, I was still an ignorant student who was very resistant to unfamiliar environments. Through slow communication and interaction, my classmates and I became very good friends. In the painting class, I learned knowledge that I had never touched before and achieved success. Through three years of hard work, I have improved in all aspects. I hope to work harder and achieve success in my future study and life.
